Marsha Rhea Williams

The first African American woman to earn a Ph.D. in Computer Science

Background

Born on August 4, 1948, in Memphis, Tennessee Nationality: African American

Education

  • 1969 - She earned her B.S. in Physics from Beloit College in Wisconsin.
  • 1971 - She earned her M.S. in Physics from the University of Michigan
  • 1976 - She earned her M.S. in systems and information science at Vanderbilt
  • 1982 - She earned her Ph.D. in computer science, making her the first African American woman to earn a Ph.D
